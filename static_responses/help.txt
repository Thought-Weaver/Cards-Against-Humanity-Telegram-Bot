Here's a list of helpful commands:

/join - Joins a pending game.
/leave - Leaves a pending game.
/listplayers - Lists everyone playing the game.
/play - Submit white card(s).
/choose - Chooses a white card to win the round.
/feedback - Inputs feedback for the bot.
/newgame - Begins a pending game of Cards Against Humanity.
/startgame - Begins the pending game.
/endgame - Ends the current game.
/decks - Lists all decks you can play with!
/adddeck - Adds the deck specified to the game.
/removedeck - Removes the deck specified from the game.
/currentdecks - Shows the current decks in play.
